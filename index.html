Netflix vs Disney+

Streaming Content Wars

Netflix has long been the global leader in online TV and movie streaming with a strong first-mover advantage. Their foresight into securing long-term streaming deals with copyright holders gave them a huge advantage early on, but now as those same copyright holders see the appetite from streaming content they are launching competing streaming services of their own.

The most noteworthy competitor to Netflix is Disney's Disney+ service, which launched in late 2019. In the chart below we can see Disney+ has had significant initial growth and is on a trajectory to compete with Netflix for the most global subscribers in the next 5 years.
