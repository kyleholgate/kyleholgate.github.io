Netflix vs Disney+

The Race for Content Creation

Netflix launched their streaming service all the way back in 2007, but they didn't release their first original movie until the end of 2014. Since that time they have significantly ramped up their movie production, far outpacing Disney. Netflix Originals now surpass Disney-released films even though Disney has been producing movies since the 1930's!

Note that the below chart only includes movies, not TV series (House of Cards was the first Netflix TV show released in February of 2013). The Disney data only includes films released under the Disney brand, not any possible subsidiaries or affiliated brands.
